About Cleonidas Ramsor

1950
Parents immigrate to New York City from Alexandria, Egypt.
1955
Cleonidas is born.
1960
Family moves to Washington, DC.
1965-1975
Interns for the Republican Party. During this time, he begins to question the wisdom of a two-party system.
1975
At the amazingly young age of 20, Cleonidas graduates from Princeton University with a phD in Political Science.
1975-1995
Junior analyst and lobbyist for the Freedom for Free pressure group.
1995
Marries daughter of Ukrainian oil tycoon.
1995-2001
As senior analyst and lobbyist for Freedom to Prosper, lobbies for increased tobacco taxes, subsidies for agribusiness and funding for oil exploration.
1997
First child born.
2001
Founds the Leonidas and Cleopatra Ramsor Memorial Foundation.
2010
Adopts three children from Eritrea.
2018
Launches Presidential campaign as an Independent.
